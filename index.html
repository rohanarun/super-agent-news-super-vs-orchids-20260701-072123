What Orchids is for — and where Super goes further

Orchids

Orchids has shown how AI assistants can live inside messaging experiences and consumer conversations, such as sports updates delivered directly to chats. It represents an experimental, lightweight approach to personal AI assistance.

Super

Super is designed for people who want a personal AI agent that operates a computer: clicking, typing, navigating browsers, and executing multi-step workflows. Its defining advantage is a reusable computer-use cache, making repeated operational work more efficient over time.
